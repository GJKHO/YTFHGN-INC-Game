Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by PlayZone ("PlayZone", "we", "us", or "our").

2. Information We Process

The website provides casual HTML5 browser games. We do not require user accounts, payments, or profile registration to play the games. Depending on your browser and hosting provider logs, the following limited information may be processed:

  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages requested, and access time.
  • Basic gameplay state stored locally in your browser, such as scores or progress where a game supports it.
  • Messages and contact details you voluntarily provide when you email us.

3. How We Use Information

We use limited information to operate the website, maintain security, respond to inquiries, fix technical issues, and understand aggregate site performance.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Some games may use browser storage to remember local scores, settings, or gameplay state. You can clear this data through your browser settings. The site may also load third-party libraries from content delivery networks used by individual game pages.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose information only when required by law, to protect our rights and website security, or to service providers that help host and operate the website.

6. Data Retention

Server logs and operational records are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for security, maintenance, legal compliance, and business purposes. Local gameplay data remains on your device until you delete it.

7. Children

The games are casual browser games and are not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.
